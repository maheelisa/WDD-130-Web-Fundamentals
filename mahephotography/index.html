Welcome to Elisa Mahe Photography

Hi! My name is Elisa and I was born and raised in Guatemala and now live in Utah, where the beauty of the mountains, forests, and changing seasons constantly inspires my work. I have always been drawn to both people and nature, and that is reflected in my photography. I focus on two main styles: Portrait and Landscape. Whether I am photographing a family, a couple, or a quiet sunset over the mountains, I try to capture something honest and beautiful. Photography has been a part of my life for over a decade. What began as a college course turned into a deep passion that has shaped the way I see the world. During my first semester studying audiovisual production, I held a professional camera for the first time and learned about composition, lighting, and visual storytelling. I instantly fell in love with the creative process behind every image. Since then, I have continued learning, practicing, and capturing meaningful moments through my lens.
